Why everyone should "Adopt Dont Shop"

  • May 2020

    Every times an animal is adopted , a space opens up in the shelter of rescue. so when you adopt dog or a cat not only are you saving that animals life, but you are also opening up a space for another animals life to be saved.

    Adopting a dog or cat is cheaper than buying one. adopting a dog is typically cheaper than purchasing one . According to prudent pet , some of the most expensive dogs include samoyeds($14,000),chow chows($11,000),Rottweilers($9000), and Afgan Hounds ($7000).
