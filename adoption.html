About adoption

  • About Pet adoption.

    Pet adoption is the process of taking responsibility for a pet that a previous owner has abandoned or released to a shelter or rescue organization.Common sources for adoptable pets are animal shelters and rescue groups . some organization give adopters ownership of the pet, while others use a guardianship model wherein the organization retains some control over the animals future use or care.

  • About online pet adoption.

    online pet adoption sites have database of pets being housed by thousands of animal shelters and rescue groups, and are searchable by the public.
