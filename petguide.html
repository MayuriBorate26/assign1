  • Pets exercise

    Pets especially dog need daily exercise to remain physically and mentally happy and healthy .but it doesnt mean that they should be a fitness freak and hit the gym everyday. It can be done just at your home ,or for that matter,wherever they are.

  • What not to feed dogs and cats

    Dogs and cats are curious by nature, particularly when it comes to food. They are also very good at begging for a taste of whatever we may be eating or cooking. As cute as they may be,though ,our pets cant always stomach the same food as us some food can be toxic and even deadly to their health.

  • Pets clothing

    Clothes can be a choking hazard if it becomes caught. Monitor your dog or cat to make sure he doesnt become overheated. A coat , sweater or costume shouldnt interfere with the movementof your pets tail, vision,hearing or when doing his business. Some dogs and cat enjoy wearing clothes but not all do
